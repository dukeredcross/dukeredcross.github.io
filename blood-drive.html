About RapidPass

RapidPass® can help save time during your next blood or platelet donation. By completing a simple 4 step process that takes about 10-15 minutes, you can skip reading and answering questions the day of your donation. Read through the pre-donation materials online, answer your health history questions and print, download or email your RapidPass® to yourself, and bring it with you.
